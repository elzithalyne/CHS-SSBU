Rules and Code of Conduct

1. No Cheating; or otherwise manipulating the game to a certain outcome.
2. No unsportsmanlike conduct towards referees, host or other players.
3. No stalling or delaying the game intentionally.
4. No inappropriate language or gestures towards host, referees or other players.
5. No violence, fighting or otherwise unethical or immoral actions.
6. No gambling on your or other player's outcomes.
7. No modded Nintendo Switch Systems.
8. No taunting or other emotes during the game.
9. You are allowed to bring your own Nintendo Switch System.
YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR YOUR OWN NINTENDO SWITCH SYSTEM.
10. You are only allowed to bring traditional controllers or personal Joy-Cons.
11 Joy-Cons and Nintendo Switch Systems will be provided.
VIOLATION OF THESE RULES WILL RESULT IN IMMEDIATE FORFEITURE AND REMOVAL FROM TOURNAMENT.
